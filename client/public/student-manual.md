# Student Manual (Attendo / UNYT)

## 1. Account Access
1. Your professor sends your account credentials (username and temporary password) by email.
2. Open the Student Portal and sign in.
3. Change your password immediately after first login.

## 2. If You Forgot Your Password
1. Contact your professor directly.
2. Your professor will reset the password and provide a new temporary one.

## 3. How to Record Attendance
1. Log in to the Student Portal.
2. Select your class/group.
3. Scan the active QR code shown by your professor.
4. Confirm your attendance status appears in your history.

## 4. If You Miss Class or Need a Day Off
1. Submit an excuse request on the platform.
2. Send an email to your professor explaining the reason.
3. Attach any supporting document if required.

## 5. Rules
1. Use only your own account.
2. Do not share login credentials.
3. Scan only active class rounds.
4. Check your attendance history regularly.
5. Report issues early to your professor.

## 6. Support
If you have a login or attendance issue:
1. Check internet connection.
2. Try logging out and in again.
3. Contact your professor with screenshot/time of the issue.
